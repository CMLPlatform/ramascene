RaMA-Scene

Raw MAterials SCENario Efficiency improvement assessment tool and blended learning package.

Open tool

Project

RaMa-Scene is an EIT Raw Materials project with the objective of facilitating decision making and the diffusion of knowledge and skills for raw materials scenarios.

Environmentally Extended Input-Output (EEIO)

EEIO is a linear economic and environmental accounting model which provides details on the relationships between economic actors (e.g. industries and final consumers) and the environmental pressures they exert as a result of their activities.
Read more

Data

The database behind the platform is EXIOBASE V3.3 (1995-2011) in Industry Technology Assumption (ITA) and product-by-product format. 200 products, 49 countries and 10 characterised impact indicators are available for selection to allow for complex multi-variate analysis.
Read more

Production and consumption views

Explore environmental and economic impact resulting from the production or consumption of goods and services. The *Production* view allows for the investigation of impacts in the production of specific product categories while in the *Final Consumption* view impacts are assessed throughout the entire supply-chain of a consumed product category.
Read more

Compare

Compare old and new results and modelled scenarios through the split view.


Modelling

Create final consumption scenarios by applying sets of relative changes to final demand. The changes are applied in sequence and results are recalculated.
